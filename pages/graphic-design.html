Frequently Asked Questions – Graphic Design

What types of graphic design services do you offer?
We offer a wide range of services including: Social media post designs (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.), Logo and brand identity design, Brochures, flyers, business cards, and packaging, Pitch decks and presentations, Reels covers, ad creatives, and digital banners. Whether you need bold marketing material or elegant branding — we've got you covered.
Do you provide custom designs or use templates?
All our designs are 100% custom-made, crafted by professional designers to reflect your brand's unique voice. We do not use generic templates — every design is made with intention, originality, and strategy.
Do you design for social media platforms?
Yes! We specialize in engaging and platform-specific social media designs, including: Instagram feed posts, reels covers, story templates, Facebook banners, event flyers, LinkedIn carousel posts and corporate content. Everything is optimized for performance and brand visibility.
Can you design a full brand identity for my business?
Absolutely. Our Brand Identity Design package includes: Logo design, Color palette & font selection, Visual mood board, Branding guidelines, Social media kit & business stationery. It's a full solution to create a consistent and premium brand presence.
Are your graphic design services affordable?
Yes! We offer budget-friendly and price-worthy packages based on your project size and timeline. Whether you're a startup or a growing brand, we make sure you get premium quality without the premium price tag.
How long will it take to complete my design?
It depends on the scope: Social media posts: 1–2 working days, Logo design: 4–5 working days, Brand identity: 7–10 working days, Brochures/Pitch decks: 3–6 working days. We always share a clear timeline before starting and stick to it.
How many revisions do I get?
We offer 2–3 rounds of revisions based on the project. Our goal is to make sure you're 100% happy with the final design while maintaining workflow efficiency.
