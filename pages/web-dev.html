Frequently Asked Questions – FAQs

What types of websites do you build?
We build a variety of websites including: Business & corporate websites, Personal branding or portfolio sites, E-commerce websites (Shopify, WooCommerce), Custom landing pages & sales funnels, Blogs and CMS-based platforms. All our websites are mobile-friendly, SEO-ready, and designed for performance.
Do you use templates or custom design?
We specialize in custom-designed websites tailored to your brand's identity and goals. No generic templates — everything is built to give your brand a unique and premium feel.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Yes! Every website we build is fully responsive and tested across mobile, tablet, and desktop. We follow a mobile-first approach to ensure a seamless experience for every visitor.
How long does it take to develop a website?
The timeline depends on the scope of your project. Basic website: 7–10 working days, Custom business website: 2–3 weeks, eCommerce or larger platforms: 3–4 weeks. We prioritize timely delivery without compromising on quality.
Are your website packages affordable?
Absolutely. We offer budget-friendly and price-worthy packages based on your needs — without hidden charges. You get premium quality at a transparent price.
Can you build an eCommerce website for my brand?
Yes! We specialize in building secure and scalable eCommerce websites with features like product listing, cart, payment integration, coupon codes, inventory management, and more — using platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, or custom builds.
Will you handle the domain and hosting too?
Yes, we can guide you through domain purchase, hosting setup, and even recommend the best platforms for performance and scalability.
Will I be able to edit my website after it's launched?
Definitely! We build websites with user-friendly dashboards (especially on WordPress and Shopify) so you can easily update content, images, or products without needing a developer.
Do you offer website maintenance or support after launch?
Yes, we offer optional monthly maintenance and support plans for updates, backups, speed optimization, and content changes. We're here even after your site goes live.
