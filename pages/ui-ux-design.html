Frequently Asked Questions – UI/UX Design

What is UI/UX design, and why is it important?
UI (User Interface) is how your app or website looks. UX (User Experience) is how users feel while navigating it. At AK Creative Studio, we combine both to ensure your users have a smooth, satisfying, and conversion-driven experience. Good UI/UX can increase engagement, reduce bounce rates, and drive better results.
What platforms do you design for?
We design for: • Websites (marketing, product, and eCommerce), • Web apps (dashboards, SaaS platforms, etc.), • Mobile apps (iOS & Android), • Landing pages & lead funnels. All our designs are mobile-first, responsive, and aligned with modern user behaviors.
What's your UI/UX design process?
Our process is structured yet flexible: 1. Discovery Call – We understand your goals, audience, and product, 2. User Flow & Wireframes – Low-fidelity structure to plan navigation, 3. UI Design – Visual interface creation using Figma, 4. Prototyping – Interactive mockups for review, 5. Developer Handoff – Exported assets + design guidelines. You'll be involved at every key stage.
Are your UI/UX services affordable?
Yes. We offer budget-friendly UI/UX design packages for startups and growth-stage brands. You get premium design and developer-ready files without burning your budget.
What tools do you use for design and collaboration?
We primarily use: Figma (for design and prototyping), Miro or Whimsical (for user flow planning), Slack/WhatsApp (for project communication), Google Docs/Sheets (for content collaboration). All designs are delivered in formats developers love.
How long does a UI/UX project take?
It depends on the scope: Single landing page: 2–3 working days, Website (5–7 pages): 7–10 days, Web/mobile app UI/UX: 2–4 weeks (based on features). We always provide a clear timeline after reviewing your requirements.
Can you redesign my existing app or website?
Absolutely! We offer UX audits and full redesigns. If your current design feels outdated or hard to use, we'll rework it to be more intuitive, modern, and conversion-focused — without disrupting your backend.
Will I get design files I can reuse or edit?
Yes. You'll receive: Figma design files, Prototypes (for demos or developer reference), Style guides (colors, fonts, spacing), Exportable assets (icons, images, illustrations). All files are yours to keep and reuse.
