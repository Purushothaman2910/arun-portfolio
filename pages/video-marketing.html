Frequently Asked Questions – Video Marketing

What is video marketing, and why does my brand need it?
Video marketing is the use of video content to promote your product, brand, or message across digital platforms. It grabs attention faster, keeps users engaged longer, and converts better than static content. Whether it's a product reel or a brand story, video brings your message to life.
Do you help with scriptwriting and content ideas?
Yes! Our team handles everything from concept to final cut, including: Scriptwriting, Storyboarding, Creative direction, Voiceovers and subtitles. We make sure your message is clear, engaging, and made for conversions.
What types of videos do you create?
We offer a wide range of video formats, including: • Instagram Reels & Facebook Shorts, • YouTube videos & YouTube Ads, • Product showcases & explainer videos, • Brand story videos & founder introductions, • Event promos & ad creatives. Every video is platform-optimized and storytelling-focused.
Can you edit videos I already have?
Absolutely! If you have raw footage or an old video, we can: Add music, text, effects, and transitions, Re-edit to suit a new platform (like Reels or YouTube Shorts), Improve pacing, flow, and brand alignment. Just send us your clips — we'll handle the rest.
Are your video marketing packages affordable?
Yes. We offer budget-friendly and price-worthy packages for startups, creators, and businesses. Whether it's one reel or a full campaign, we provide premium quality at transparent pricing.
How long does it take to deliver a video?
Timelines vary by scope: Reels/Shorts: 2–3 working days, Product videos: 3–5 working days, Brand films: 5–7 working days. We always share timelines upfront and deliver on schedule.
Do you include voiceovers, subtitles, and music?
Yes. All our video packages can include: • Professional voiceovers (in English, Tamil, or any preferred language), • Captions/subtitles for better reach, • Background music and sound design, • Logo reveals and CTA screens.
How many revisions are included?
We include 2–3 free revision rounds, depending on your package. Our goal is to ensure you're fully satisfied while keeping turnaround times efficient.
